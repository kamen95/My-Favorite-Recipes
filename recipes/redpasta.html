Red Pasta

What is Red Pasta

it is one of the traditional italian cuisine recipe known for its, taste, flavour and creaminess in each bite. the recipe is very much inspired from the italian cuisine but a tailored and customised recipe for indian taste buds.

Ingredients for making the tomato Sauce

  • 5-6 Tomatoes (diced)
  • 1 Garlic clove
  • 1 Onion
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 4-5 Basil leaves
  • 1tbsp Onion (Chopped)
  • 1/2 tbsp Garlic (Chopped)
  • Salt
  • OLive Oil

Ingredients for the Pasta

  • 110 grams of Pasta
  • 3 cups water
  • a pinch of salth

Steps to prepare Red Sauce Pasta

  1. Add garlic cloves, onion and bay leaf to it.
  2. Add water and then season with salt and sugar.
  3. Cover and let the tomatoes boil. Cool and then grind to a puree.
  4. Now, in a separate pan, heat oil and add chopped onions and chopped garlic.
  5. Add the tomato puree. Cook till its reduces to half. Add basil leaves and keep aside.
  6. Boil water with salt. Add the pasta to it and let it boil. Drain when done
  7. Transfer it to a serving dish and serve with the tomato sauce.
