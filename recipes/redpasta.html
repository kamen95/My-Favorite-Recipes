Red Pasta

What is Red Pasta

it is one of the traditional italian cuisine recipe known for its, taste, flavour and creaminess in each bite. the recipe is very much inspired from the italian cuisine but a tailored and customised recipe for indian taste buds.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomoates
  • Olive Oil
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Herbs and Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Make tomato paste out of the tomatoes
